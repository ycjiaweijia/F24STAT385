Example 1

X[1,]
#> a b c d 
#> 1 2 3 4

Meaning: the first row of X

Example 2
X[, c(1, "c")]

X[, c("a", "c")]

#> [3,] 9 11

# OR
X[, c(1, 3)]
#>      a  c
#> [1,] 1  3
#> [2,] 5  7
#> [3,] 9 11
